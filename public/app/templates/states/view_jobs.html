Skill Justification
Candidates’ skill levels require specific justification, to include a quantification of their experience, as well as a detailed description of their relevant skills. This section should be written as prose and must demonstrate the candidate’s satisfaction of the position requirements. In addition to justifying the candidate’s experience level, this section should be used as an opportunity to market the candidate by highlighting his/her relevant skills. Each Skill Level Justification section should contain the following components:
Introductory sentence stating candidate’s skill level
Brief description (1-3 sentences) describing candidate’s area(s) of expertise
Sentence entailing any relevant education/certifications the candidate has
